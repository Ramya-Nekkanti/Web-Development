"Our College Canteen Management System" facilitates everyone to
order the food online from anywhere in the college. To do this one has to login to the website with their valid credentials and check for the availability of food. After selecting the item, payment can be processed online. Once the food is ready for pickup the user will receive the notification. This way it reduces a lot of time for an individual during the busy hours.
